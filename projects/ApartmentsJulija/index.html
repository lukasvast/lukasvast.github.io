About us:

Vacation apartments located at the coast of North Adriatic Sea. We have 12 apartments available that can accommodate from 2 to 6 people in each apartment. All of our guests have free parking, free wireless internet access, international satellite tv access and access to outside grill area. Our sea facing appartments are all air conditioned. We are very close to the beach and restaurants which are only 100 meters away. Also city center of Crikvenica is within a 1 kilometer walking distance from the house.
