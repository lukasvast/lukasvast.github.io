Welcome to Apartments Julija

A Family Tradition Since the 1990s

Welcome to our family-owned vacation home on the North Adriatic Coast. With over 30 years of experience, we're proud to offer a welcoming atmosphere perfect for families, couples, and friends looking for a comfortable stay in Crikvenica.

We offer 9 fully equipped apartments accommodating 2-6 guests, all with free Wi-Fi, international satellite TV, and air conditioning. Enjoy our self-service BBQ area with complimentary wood and grilling equipment.

Flexible Self Check-In/Out: Check-in anytime after 14:00, check-out by 10:00. Limited on-site parking available (6 spaces), but free public parking is right in front and behind the house - you'll easily find a spot! Best part? Once parked, you won't need your car - the beach is just a 5-minute walk away, a small market is nearby, and there's even a charming tourist "train" station right in front of our house!
