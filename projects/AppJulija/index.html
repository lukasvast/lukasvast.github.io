Welcome to Apartments Julija

Experience the Adriatic Dream

Vacation apartments located at the coast of North Adriatic Sea. We have 8 apartments available that can accommodate from 2 to 4 people in each apartment.

All of our guests have free wireless internet access, international satellite TV access, and access to an outside grill area. Our apartments are all air conditioned.

We are very close to the beach and restaurants which are within a 5 minutes walking distance. The city center of Crikvenica is within a 1 kilometer walking distance from the house.
